Toyota Camry (2018-2020)
Turn Off the Vehicle
Ensure the ignition is off and remove the key.

Locate the Battery
Open the hood and find the battery near the driverâ€™s side firewall.

Disconnect Battery Cables
Use a 10mm wrench to loosen and remove the negative cable (black). Repeat for the positive cable (red).

Remove the Battery Hold-Down Bracket
Unscrew the clamp securing the battery in place. Set the hardware aside.

Lift Out the Old Battery
Carefully remove the battery, noting its orientation.

Inspect and Clean Connections
Use a wire brush to remove corrosion from terminals.

Insert the New Battery
Place a Group 24F battery (recommended type) into the tray, ensuring proper polarity.

Reconnect the Hold-Down Bracket
Secure the battery using the previously removed bracket.

Reconnect Battery Cables
Attach the positive cable (red) first, followed by the negative cable (black). Tighten securely.

Test the Installation
Start the vehicle to ensure proper function.
