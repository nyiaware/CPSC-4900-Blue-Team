2018-2020 Toyota Corolla
Preparation

Ensure the vehicle is parked on a flat surface and the parking brake is engaged. Slightly loosen the lug nuts.
Jack Up the Car

Use a jack to lift one corner at a time and secure it with jack stands.
Remove Tires

Take off the lug nuts and remove the wheels.
Rotation Pattern

For FWD Corolla: Front tires go to the back; rear tires cross to the front.
Reinstall Tires

Place the tires on their new positions, screw on the lug nuts, and tighten by hand.
Lower and Torque

Lower the vehicle and tighten the lug nuts to the recommended 76 ft-lbs.
Inspect and Test

Inspect all tires for wear or damage and perform a brief drive to ensure proper installation.
