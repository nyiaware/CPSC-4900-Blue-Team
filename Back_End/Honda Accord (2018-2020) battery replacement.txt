Honda Accord (2018-2020)
Turn Off the Vehicle

Turn off the ignition and remove the key.
Locate the Battery

Open the hood and find the battery at the front center of the engine bay.
Disconnect the Battery Cables

Use a 10mm wrench to disconnect the negative cable first, then the positive cable.
Remove the Battery Hold-Down Clamp

Unscrew the bolts securing the clamp.
Lift Out the Old Battery

Carefully remove the old battery, noting its orientation.
Inspect and Clean Connections

Clean the terminals and tray to remove corrosion.
Insert the New Battery

Place a Group 24F battery in the tray, ensuring the correct orientation.
Reattach the Hold-Down Clamp

Tighten the clamp to keep the battery stable.
Reconnect the Cables

Attach the positive cable first, then the negative cable. Tighten them securely.
Verify the Replacement

Start the vehicle and ensure all systems are functioning properly.
